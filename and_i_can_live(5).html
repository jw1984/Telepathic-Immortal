Telepathic Wind Robot

A fictional cyber-mystic sound visualizer. It reacts to sound levels and programmed rules; it does not detect telepathy, thoughts, emotions, consciousness, truthfulness, identity, health, or intent.

Microphone and privacy notice

The application requests microphone access only after you select Start Microphone. Its code processes changing sound values temporarily in browser memory to draw the animation.

The application is not designed to record, save, upload, or transmit microphone audio, and the microphone processing chain is not connected to your speakers. Your website host and browser may still process ordinary connection, permission, and usage information under their own policies.

Washington law generally requires consent before intercepting or recording a private conversation. Do not activate the microphone where another person’s private conversation may be captured unless everyone involved has agreed. This notice is general information, not legal advice.

Important use limits

The labels, movement, poetry, and “reactivity” score are fictional animation outputs. Do not use this artwork for surveillance, recording conversations, identifying speakers, making health or mental-state decisions, or making claims about another person.

Selecting Stop Microphone, hiding or leaving the page, or closing the page stops the microphone tracks controlled by this application. Browser permission may remain remembered until you change it in browser settings, but the application will no longer have an active microphone stream.
